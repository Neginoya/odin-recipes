Korean Salt Bread(Sogeum-Ppang)

Sogeum-ppang translates to "salt bread" in Korean. This viarl South Korean Pastry is known for its crispy exterior, pillowy-soft, butter-rich interior, and a finishing touch of savory salt. This hybrid between croissant, dinner roll, and milk bread has become a staple in Korean bakeries and cafes.

Ingredients

Tangzhong for dough

  • 14g flour
  • 60ml water
  • 155ml full fat whole milk
  • 8g fine salt

Dough

  • 310g bread flour
  • 40g sugar
  • 7g dried yeast
  • 30g salted butter, softened and cubed
  • 200g cold butter(cut into 8x25g pieces)
  • flaked salt to garnish at end
