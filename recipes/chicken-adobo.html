Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a beloved, classic Filipino dish featuring chicken braised in a savory, tangy sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic, seasoned with bay leaves and black peppercorns, creating a rich, balanced flavor profile that's salty, sour, and slightly sweet, perfect served with rice. It's known for its tender, fall-off-the-bone meat and a glossy sauce that reduces to a delicious glaze, with variations including drier or stew-like versions
